Summary

Tappy Toons does not collect personal data. The app works fully offline and does not communicate with external servers during normal use.

1. Data Collection

The application does not collect personal data.

We do not access or collect:

  • Your name, email, or identity information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Persistent device identifiers (such as IMEI or advertising ID)
  • Account credentials or passwords

2. Local Log Files

The application may store limited technical log files locally on your device for troubleshooting purposes. These logs may include:

  • Device model and Android OS version
  • Application activity timestamps (e.g., start/stop events)
  • Error messages, if they occur

These logs:

  • Are stored only on your device
  • Are retained for a limited time (up to 24 hours)
  • Are not transmitted automatically
  • Are not accessible to the developer unless you choose to share them

You may manually send logs (for example, via email) when requesting support. By choosing to do so, you consent to sharing this information with the developer.

Logs are not intended to include personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or precise location.

3. No Third-Party Services

Tappy Toons does not use analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking technologies.

The application does not integrate SDKs such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services.

4. Children’s Privacy

This application is designed for young children.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to minimize data usage and protect children's privacy.

5. Brazil — LGPD

This application is designed to comply with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018).

Since the application does not collect personal data, no personal data is processed, stored, or shared by the developer.

6. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, correct, or delete.

If you have any privacy-related questions or concerns, you may contact the developer.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Continued use of the application after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
